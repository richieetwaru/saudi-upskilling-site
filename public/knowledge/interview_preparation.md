# Interview Preparation — Saudi Job Market

## General Tips
- Research the company's Vision 2030 alignment and Saudization initiatives
- Prepare examples of how your skills align with national development goals
- Dress formally — Saudi corporate culture values professional appearance
- Be punctual — arrive 10-15 minutes early
- Know your salary expectations in SAR — research market rates on Bayt.com and LinkedIn

## Technology Roles

### Cloud Engineer
- Difficulty: Medium-Hard
- Key questions: Explain VPC architecture, Describe a migration you led, How do you handle cost optimization in AWS, What's the shared responsibility model
- Tips: Prepare a whiteboard architecture diagram, Know the difference between regions/AZs, Have a cost savings example ready
- Common mistakes: Not knowing Saudi data residency requirements, Ignoring security best practices

### Data Analyst
- Difficulty: Medium
- Key questions: Walk through your SQL query optimization process, How do you handle missing data, Describe a dashboard you built that drove business decisions, What's the difference between supervised and unsupervised learning
- Tips: Bring portfolio examples, Know Arabic data sources (GASTAT, Saudi Open Data), Practice live SQL
- Common mistakes: Not connecting analysis to business outcomes, Weak storytelling with data

### Cybersecurity Specialist
- Difficulty: Hard
- Key questions: Describe your incident response process, How would you secure a cloud-native application, What compliance frameworks are relevant in KSA (NCA ECC), Explain zero trust architecture
- Tips: Know NCA Essential Cybersecurity Controls, Prepare a real incident response example, Understand Saudi data protection law (PDPL)
- Common mistakes: Being too theoretical, Not knowing local regulations

### Full Stack Developer
- Difficulty: Medium
- Key questions: Design a REST API for a given scenario, Explain your testing strategy, How do you handle state management, Walk through a performance optimization you did
- Tips: Have a GitHub portfolio ready, Know Arabic/RTL layout considerations, Practice system design
- Common mistakes: Not asking clarifying questions, Jumping to code without planning

## Healthcare Roles

### Health Informatics Specialist
- Difficulty: Medium
- Key questions: Experience with EHR systems, HL7/FHIR standards knowledge, How to ensure patient data privacy under Saudi regulations
- Tips: Know Saudi NPHIES (National Platform for Health Insurance Exchange), Understand MOH digital transformation goals

## Finance Roles

### FinTech Product Manager
- Difficulty: Medium-Hard
- Key questions: How do you prioritize features, Experience with SAMA (Saudi Central Bank) regulations, Describe a product launch, How do you measure success
- Tips: Know Saudi payment landscape (mada, STC Pay, SADAD), Understand open banking regulations

## Behavioral Questions (All Roles)
- Tell me about a time you led a team under pressure
- Describe a conflict with a colleague and how you resolved it
- How do you stay current with industry trends
- What motivates you to work in Saudi Arabia
- How would you contribute to our Saudization goals

## Saudi Workplace Culture
- Hierarchy is respected — address seniors formally
- Building personal relationships matters before business
- Meeting times may be flexible — be adaptable
- Arabic greetings are appreciated even if interview is in English
- Friday-Saturday weekend — Sunday is a workday
