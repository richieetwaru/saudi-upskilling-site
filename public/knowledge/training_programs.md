# Training Programs — Saudi Arabia

## Government-Backed Programs

### Saudi Digital Academy (SDA)
- Provider: Communications & IT Ministry — Duration: 3-6 months — Format: Online + Bootcamp — Cost: Free — Level: Beginner-Intermediate — Certificate: Yes
- Programs: Data Science, Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing, AI/ML, UX Design
- Eligibility: Saudi nationals, age 18+
- Modules: Foundations, Hands-on Projects, Capstone, Career Prep

### Tuwaiq Academy
- Provider: Saudi Federation for Cybersecurity (SAFCSP) — Duration: 3-12 months — Format: In-person (Riyadh) + Online — Cost: Free — Level: Beginner-Advanced — Certificate: Yes
- Programs: Full Stack Development, iOS Development, Cybersecurity, Data Science, AI, Cloud
- Eligibility: Saudi nationals, varies by program
- Modules: Technical Foundations, Specialization Track, Industry Project, Placement Support

### Doroob (HRDF Platform)
- Provider: Human Resources Development Fund — Duration: Self-paced — Format: Online — Cost: Free — Level: All levels — Certificate: Yes
- Programs: 5000+ courses across business, technology, soft skills, healthcare
- Eligibility: All Saudi nationals
- Modules: Video lessons, Assessments, Completion certificates

### Tamheer (On-the-Job Training)
- Provider: HRDF — Duration: 3-6 months — Format: On-site placement — Cost: Free (3000 SAR/mo stipend) — Level: Fresh graduates — Certificate: Yes
- Programs: Workplace training at top companies (Aramco, STC, SABIC, banks)
- Eligibility: Saudi graduates with no prior work experience
- Modules: Workplace orientation, Mentor-guided tasks, Performance review

### Hafiz (Job Search Support)
- Provider: HRDF — Duration: Up to 12 months — Format: Online — Cost: Free (2000 SAR/mo allowance) — Level: Job seekers
- Programs: Job search skills, Resume writing, Interview preparation
- Eligibility: Unemployed Saudi nationals aged 20-35

## Private & International Programs

### Udacity Nanodegrees (MCIT Partnership)
- Provider: Udacity + Saudi MCIT — Duration: 3-4 months — Format: Online — Cost: Subsidized (up to 75% HRDF) — Level: Intermediate — Certificate: Yes
- Programs: Data Engineering, AI Programming, Cloud DevOps, Digital Marketing
- Modules: Lessons, Projects (mentor-reviewed), Career services

### Coursera for Campus (Saudi Universities)
- Provider: Coursera — Duration: Self-paced — Format: Online — Cost: Free for enrolled students — Level: All — Certificate: Yes
- Programs: Google Career Certificates, IBM Professional Certificates, Meta certifications

### AWS re/Start (Saudi Chapter)
- Provider: Amazon Web Services — Duration: 12 weeks — Format: Online cohort — Cost: Free — Level: Beginner — Certificate: AWS Cloud Practitioner exam voucher
- Programs: Cloud fundamentals, Linux, networking, security, Python
- Eligibility: No prior IT experience required

### Google Career Certificates
- Provider: Google (via Coursera) — Duration: 3-6 months — Format: Online — Cost: Subsidized — Level: Beginner — Certificate: Yes
- Programs: Data Analytics, Cybersecurity, IT Support, UX Design, Project Management
