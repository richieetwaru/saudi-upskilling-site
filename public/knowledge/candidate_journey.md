# Candidate Journey — Saudi Upskilling Intelligence

## The 7 Stages

### Stage 1: Sign Up & Onboarding
- Candidate registers on the platform
- Profile creation: name, contact, education, work experience
- Visual account approval status
- Card: `onboarding` with step checklist
- Trigger: First interaction, "get started", "sign up", "create profile"

### Stage 2: Profile & CV Upload
- Upload CV (text-based parsing)
- AI detects skills, experience, education
- Visual profile completion percentage
- Card: `onboarding` showing profile completion steps
- Trigger: "upload CV", "complete profile", "my profile"

### Stage 3: AI Coach & Learning Buddy
- Magic becomes the candidate's AI coach from sign-up
- Scripted guided experience through the platform
- Voice + text interactions
- Personalized recommendations based on profile
- Card: `coach` with tips, encouragement, next actions
- Trigger: "what should I do next", "help me", "advice", "coach"

### Stage 4: Skill Assessment
- Skills baseline report based on CV and self-assessment
- Gap analysis comparing current skills to job requirements
- Role recommendations based on skill match
- Card: `assessment` with score ring and skill bars
- Trigger: "assess my skills", "skill gap", "what am I good at", "test my skills"

### Stage 5: Interview Coordination
- Interview scheduling (date, time, format)
- AI-generated feedback draft after interviews
- Preparation tips specific to role and company
- Cards: `interview` for tips + `schedule` for appointments
- Trigger: "prepare for interview", "interview tips", "my interview", "schedule"

### Stage 6: Contract & Offer
- Offer creation and approval flow
- HRDF funding application ("Send for funding" action)
- Status tracking (Sent, Approved, Pending)
- Offer tracking dashboard
- Card: `offer` with salary, status, benefits, accept button
- Trigger: "my offer", "job offer", "contract", "HRDF funding"

### Stage 7: Training & Learner Journey
- Training enrollment confirmation
- Skills being developed tracking
- Narrative linkage: employer action → learner growth
- Milestone tracking and progress visualization
- Cards: `training` for programs + `progress` for journey tracking
- Trigger: "my training", "progress", "how am I doing", "learning journey"

## Pre-Select Card Sequences

These are curated carousel flows the agent can use:

- **discovery**: tile-grid (sectors) → job → job → job
- **upskill**: assessment → skill → training → training
- **interview-prep**: interview → interview → schedule
- **full-journey**: onboarding → assessment → skill → training → interview → offer → progress
- **jobs-in-tech**: job → job → job → job (all tech sector)
- **coach-session**: coach → assessment → skill → coach
